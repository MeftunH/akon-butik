## Product range

The current site is a maintenance page, so the on-site category tree was not browsable. Recoverable signals:

- **A product-category URL pattern is alive in Google's index:** `/product-category/ustgiyim/elbise/` ([elbise category](https://www.akonbutik.com/product-category/ustgiyim/elbise/)) — the slugs `ustgiyim` ("upper-wear") and `elbise` ("dress") confirm a WooCommerce-style taxonomy organised around women's outerwear and dresses. (Page returns 404 today — taxonomy was published, then taken down for the e-commerce relaunch.)
- **Earlier search-engine snapshots described the catalogue** as: pants, sweatpants, shorts, dresses, suits (takım), and t-shirts. Source: aggregated description in Bing/Google result excerpt for [akonbutik.com](https://www.akonbutik.com/).
- **Instagram captions** focus on **"kombin"** (full outfits) for women, including a "Spor" (sport / casual-athletic) line — see [@akonbutik reel DAx-cflAqgN](https://www.instagram.com/akonbutik/reel/DAx-cflAqgN/).
- **Audience addressed:** "Selam kızlar" — explicitly women / girls. No mention of men's or kids.
- **No tesettür / modest-fashion language detected** in the captions we could read — the brand presents as mainstream women's casual & smart-casual, not specifically modest-wear (separating it from the dominant Adapazarı butik category, e.g. İmaj Butik, Saklı Tesettür).

**Inferred positioning:** trend-driven, mid-market women's casual & smart-casual ready-to-wear, organised around weekly outfit (kombin) drops. Likely sizes: standard Turkish women's range (S–XL); plus-size not signalled.

## Brand voice signals

From the only two verbatim public-facing post openings:

1. > "Selam kızlar🫶🏻 Çok şık bir kombin ile geldik😍 …" ([source](https://www.instagram.com/akonbutik/p/DClxJa5gzL9/))
2. > "Selam kızlar🤍 Keyifli pazarlar diliyoruz🫶🏻 Spor …" ([source](https://www.instagram.com/akonbutik/reel/DAx-cflAqgN/))

Adjectives & tonal markers the brand uses **about itself**:

- **şık** (chic / stylish) — self-applied to outfits
- **kombin** (curated outfit) — central organising idea
- **keyifli** (pleasant / enjoyable) — pleasantries to customers

Tonal pattern:

- Opens every post with a warm second-person greeting to "kızlar".
- Heavy use of soft / pastel emoji: 🫶🏻 🤍 😍 — never red-heart / loud emoji.
- Tone is **friendly, peer-like, encouraging** — "we came with…", "we wish you…" — not aspirational or distant.
- No discount-shouting language; no `%`, `İNDİRİM`, capslock detected in the indexed snippets.

Brand tone summary: **warm, peer-to-peer, soft-feminine, weekly-outfit boutique**. Closer to a friendly Sakarya neighbourhood Instagram-shop voice than to a luxury or aspirational positioning.

3. **Owner / founder name** — not public anywhere. Do not write a "founder's story" without input.
4. **Second store (Serdivan)** — Facebook tag exists, but no current-day evidence of an active second location. Do not list a second address.
5. **Landline / WhatsApp Business number** — only the personal-mobile-format number (0533…) is published. Confirm with operator whether to surface this on the storefront's contact page or replace with a business landline.
6. **VKN / Mersis No** — not on the site; required for KVKK / Mesafeli Satış Sözleşmesi pages once the e-commerce store goes live. Must come from the operator.

8. **Real product taxonomy** — "ustgiyim/elbise" was the only category slug we could see live; the full DIA-driven category tree should be the source of truth, not the legacy WordPress slugs.

   > Display once, format with spaces (Turkish convention), and label it as "Mağaza / WhatsApp" only after confirming with the operator. Do not invent a landline.

3. **Email — verified:** `info@akonbutik.com`. Use this on Contact, Footer, and as the KVKK / iletişim address.

4. **Brand-voice guideline for the new About / Hero copy:**
   - Open with a greeting in the same register as their Instagram: warm, peer-to-peer, addressed to women. e.g. "Sakarya'nın kalbinden, sizin için seçtiğimiz kombinler."
   - Lean on the words **kombin**, **şık**, **keyifli** — these are the brand's own.
   - Avoid luxury / aspirational vocabulary ("lüks", "premium", "zarafet") — that is **not** how the brand talks about itself in the only public copy that exists.

5. **About page — what we can safely write today (no fabrication):**
